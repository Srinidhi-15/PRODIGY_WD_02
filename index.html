Tic Tac Toe

Welcome to Tic-Tac-Toe! Click on a box to start the game.

Rules and Regulations:

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  • Players take turns marking empty cells with "X" or "O".
  • The first player to get three of their marks in a row wins.
  • If the entire grid is filled without a winner, the game is a draw.
